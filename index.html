DIMUN VIII About Us

Honourable MUN Directors, esteemed delegates, respected committee Chairs and welcome guests,

DIMUN is back! The eighth annual Dulwich International Model United Nations conference (DIMUN VIII) will be held at Dulwich College Beijing from 2nd of March to 4th of March.

Model United Nations is a unique opportunity for students to expand their knowledge of world affairs and improve their public speaking skills through research, discussion and debate with other international students who share the same passions.

At DIMUN VIII, Key Stage 3 (middle school) delegates will be debating a total of 32 topics in eight different committees. The conference will be supported by experienced Key Stage 4/5 (high school) Chairs and the DIMUNITE press team, led by Secretaries-General Jackel Cheung and Sunho Kwon.

This website will contain the most updated information on the conference, a Q&A Section where you can get live support during the conference, as well as preparatory resources for schools and delegates – both old and new – for DIMUN. Please do not hesitate to contact us for more information, or any questions regarding the conference at dimun@dulwich-beijing.cn.
