  Hotels

Crowne Plaza:

Crowne Plaza International Airport Beijing offers 5-star rooms and 24-hour service. It is located just 6.7 km away from the Beijing International Airport, and provides a free shuttle bus every 30 minutes with advance reservation. It is also directly opposite to the China International Exhibition Centre and a 30-minute drive from the city centre.

Address: 60 Fuqian First Street, Beijing International Airport Area, Shunyi Disctrict, 101312 Shunyi, China

CITIC Hotel:

Located just a few minutes from the Beijing Capital Airport, the hotel offers shuttle free airport, city and exhibition shuttle services. Guests have access to a recreation centre, an indoor and outdoor pool, and several international restaurants and casual cafes.

Address: No.9 Xiao Tianzhu Road, Capital International Airport, Beijing, China, 100621
