Human Rights Council



The Human Rights Council acts to safeguard the rights of all based on the United Nations declaration of human rights.
The chairs for this council are; Heather Leung and Joy Ryu.

The topics for this committee are;

  • Measures to protect the movement of refugees
  • Measures to combat international human trafficking
  • Addressing the issue of child labourers in Asia
  • Measures to rehabilitate internally displaced persons
