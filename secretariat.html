Secretariat

Student Executive Team

The DIMUN conference is organised and run by an organising team made entirely of students, with the support of Dulwich College Beijing and MUN Directors. Leading this team and DIMUN X are the Executive Student Officers below:



Secretary General

Yi Xin Lee

Deputy Secretary General:Yi Xin Lee

Honorable student officers, fellow delegates, and most esteemed guests,

Welcome to DIMUN X!

My name is Yi Xin Lee and I am incredibly honoured to be serving as your Secretary General for DIMUN X. For the last four years or so, I have been an active part of the MUN community, from debating resolutions as a delegate, to running committees as a chair and now, overseeing DIMUN as a member of the core team. When I first started doing MUN in Year 9, I was in the Environment Commission and, like most new delegates, was terrified. Nevertheless, after doing multiple MUNs throughout the years, I have developed an incredibly drive for MUN — to any new delegates reading this, I hope to pass on this passion to you too! Hence, I strongly urge everyone to actively participate so that we can all work together to create an enjoyable and fruitful conference.

Deputy Secretary General

Ethan Zhou

Deputy Secretary General: Ethan Zhou

Esteemed directors, honorable chairs and respected delegates, My name is Ethan Zhou and it is my greatest pleasure to serve as your Deputy Secretary-General for DIMUN X. Since entering the MUN community in 2017, I have participated in debating, chairing, now administering DIMUN as a core team member.

Among all the conferences I have been to, my experience in DIMUN has been the most valuable. Like all of you, I was once a beginner in MUN. Through DIMUN, I have gained invaluable friendships and developed my interest in debating. To any new delegates: don't be afraid to step out of your comfort zone and enjoy this extraordinary opportunity!

Whether you are a proficient delegate, a beginner, or a prospective committee chair, DIMUN will be the best opportunity to debate with like-minded individuals and appreciate the realm of politics. I am confident that our team will provide you with fruitful debates which will pave your way as a future leader.
