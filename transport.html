Transport

Transport

The conference will take place in Dulwich College Beijing starting from March 1st 2019.

Dulwich College Beijing

The conference will take place at Dulwich College Beijing, a British International school located in Shunyi.

Buses to and from Dulwich College Beijing will be available for outside-of-Beijing school delegations staying at hotels. Transporation to and from the airport or train station will need to be arranged with the hotel the delegation is staying at.

Location: 北京市顺义区首都机场路89号
